M & L

  • M x y: Move pen to (x, y)
  • M x1,y1[ x2,y2[…]]: Move pen to (x1, y1), then (x2, y2), …
  • M x1 y1 L x2 y2[ x3 y3[…]], M x1 y1 L x2,y2[ x3,y3[…]]: Draw linearly from (x1, y1) to (x2, y2), then (x3, y3), …
